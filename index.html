Our Services

Our Services

All treatments are personalised and focused on your individual recovery and health goals.

Breast Cancer Rehabilitation

Compassionate, specialist rehabilitation for patients recovering from breast cancer treatment, surgery, and related side effects.

Women's Health Physical Therapy

Specialized physical therapy addressing the unique health needs of women across all stages of life.

Pelvic Health Therapy

Expert care for pelvic floor dysfunction, incontinence, pelvic pain, and pre/post-natal recovery.

Orthopedic Physical Therapy

Evidence-based treatment for musculoskeletal conditions, joint pain, and movement dysfunction.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured recovery programs following surgery to restore strength, mobility, and full function safely.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to reduce pain, improve mobility, and restore normal movement patterns.

Pain Management Therapy

Integrative, personalized approaches to managing chronic and acute pain for long-term relief.

Movement & Mobility Therapy

Targeted therapy to improve movement quality, flexibility, and confidence in daily activities.

How We Can Help You

We help patients who are experiencing:

  • Pain or stiffness
  • Recovery after breast cancer treatment
  • Pelvic health issues
  • Back, neck, or joint pain
  • Post-surgery recovery
  • Mobility problems
  • Muscle weakness
  • Balance problems
